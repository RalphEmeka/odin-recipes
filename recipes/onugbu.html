Onugbu

Onugbu is an Igbo delicacy dating more than five hundred years. its main ingredience is the Palm fruit. Fish and assorted meat are normally used to ganish it

Ingredients

  • Palm Oil
  • Smoked fish
  • Assorted meat
  • Bitter leaves
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil palm oil
  2. Add onions, crayfish and Pepper
  3. Parboil the fish and meat
  4. Pour both on the same pot and cook for 30 minutes
  5. add ingridients to taste
  6. wash and cut bitter leaves to small sizes
  7. add the cut leaves and boil for 2 minutes
  8. Food is Ready
